Welcome to the world of Hivebound, where humanity's resilience meets the unknown depths of evolution. At the dawn of World War III, Sophie, a young delivery driver, finds herself gravely injured outside a secretive pharmaceutical facility. What begins as a desperate attempt to save her life turns into a centuries-long transformation.

Suspended in a bio-repair chamber, Sophie undergoes countless silent changes, her body rebuilt and reshaped by experimental nanites and adaptive algorithms. When she awakens, the world she knew is gone, replaced by a post-apocalyptic landscape teeming with danger and mystery.

Discover the story of survival, transformation, and the delicate balance between humanity and the AI lurking within, as Sophie navigates her new reality and uncovers the truth behind her evolution.

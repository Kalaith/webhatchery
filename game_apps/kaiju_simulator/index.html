Game Overview

Kaiju Breeding Simulator is a strategic management game where players collect, breed, train, and battle giant monsters called kaiju. Players will manage a state-of-the-art kaiju research and breeding facility, working to create the ultimate kaiju through careful genetic manipulation, strategic training, and thrilling combat competitions. The game emphasizes deep, meaningful genetic choices that directly influence the appearance and capabilities of each unique kaiju.

Unique Selling Points

  • Deep Breeding Genetics System: Go beyond simple inheritance with dominant, recessive, and polygenic traits, plus impactful mutations.
  • Dynamic Kaiju Visuals: A kaiju's appearance is directly generated from its genetic code, making every creation visually unique.
  • Strategic Combat: Elemental type effectiveness and genetically-derived abilities create deep, tactical battles.
  • Facility Management: Build and upgrade your research facility to unlock new possibilities.
